Hi, my name is Jonatan!

Sometimes I find myself in need of knowing the address of my physical location. Therefore I made this website for finding out your current address based on your GPS position.

The website will ask for permission to know your position, which it'll use to determine your address You can also fine-tune the position by panning the map.

This website was built using Flask and Leaflet. In the process of building this website, I created two Leaflet plugins that I've open sourced: Leaflet UserMarker and Leaflet CenterMarker.
